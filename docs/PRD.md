# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## 1. Product Vision
FreightFlow is a unified Freight Marketplace and Transportation Management System (TMS). Its goal is to eliminate manual friction in freight matching, dispatching, execution, and document generation (PODs, Invoicing) by connecting Shippers, Brokers, and Carriers on a single digital platform.

## 2. Key Objectives
*   **Centralized Freight Matching**: Allow Shippers to post loads to a public marketplace or tender directly to trusted partner networks.
*   **Real-Time Execution Tracking**: Equip Drivers with a mobile-optimized interface to provide live GPS updates and status changes.
*   **Automated Document Generation**: Remove paper-based PODs by capturing digital receiver signatures and automatically generating unalterable PDF Proof of Delivery documents.
*   **Strict Security & Multi-Tenancy**: Ensure users only see data relevant to their specific role within their specific company context.

## 3. Core Features
1.  **Load Origination**: Shippers/Brokers create loads with specific origin/destination parameters, equipment requirements, and commodity details.
2.  **Marketplace & Bidding**: Carriers/Owner-Operators view a live load board, submit competitive bids, and negotiate pricing.
3.  **Tendering & Assignments**: Direct, private routing of loads to preferred partners, bypassing the public marketplace.
4.  **Shipment Execution**: The physical movement of freight, tracked via `DISPATCHED`, `IN_TRANSIT`, `DELIVERED` status updates.
5.  **Digital POD & Exceptions**: Driver capture of OS&D (Over, Short, Damaged) notes alongside electronic signatures.
6.  **Fleet Management**: Carrier dispatchers manage trucks, trailers, and driver availability.
7.  **Financial Settlements**: Invoicing, line-item deductions, and broker margin calculations.

## 4. Target Audience
*   **Shippers**: Seeking visibility and reliable transport.
*   **Brokers**: Seeking to match shipper demand with carrier capacity while maximizing margin.
*   **Carriers (Dispatchers & Admins)**: Seeking to keep their trucks loaded, drivers paid, and reduce empty miles.
*   **Drivers & Owner-Operators**: Seeking a simple, non-intrusive mobile interface to manage their daily runs.
